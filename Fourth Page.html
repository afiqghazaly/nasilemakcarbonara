Our Hometown

Kajang is a town in southeastern Selangor, Malaysia. It is located 20 km southeast of downtown Kuala Lumpur, in the coterminous mukim (commune) of Kajang in the Hulu Langat District.

Kajang is served by a network of tolled expressways and federal highways.

Kajang is famous for its sate, a form of skewered barbecued meat. Informally, Kajang is known as the Sate Town.
